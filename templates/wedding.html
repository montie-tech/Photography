Wedding Photography Package

  1. Pre-Wedding Consultation
    • Meeting (in-person or virtual) to discuss the wedding theme, timeline, and preferred photography style.
    • Location scouting and planning (optional).
  2. Coverage

    Choose based on package level:

    • Basic Package: 4–5 hours coverage (ceremony & portraits)
    • Standard Package: 8 hours coverage (preparation, ceremony, reception)
    • Premium Package: Full-day coverage (morning preparations to late reception)
    • Option to add a second photographer or videographer.
  3. Deliverables
    • Edited Photos: Professionally retouched high-resolution images.
    • Online Gallery: Password-protected link for easy viewing and download.
    • USB Drive / Cloud Delivery: Backup of all final edited photos.
    • Photo Album (optional): Premium printed photo book.
    • Highlight Slideshow: A short cinematic slideshow with music (optional).
  4. Add-ons
    • Engagement / pre-wedding photoshoot
    • Drone photography
    • Same-day photo highlights for display at reception
    • Prints and wall art packages
    • Wedding video coverage
  5. Turnaround Time
    • Preview gallery within 1–2 weeks
    • Full gallery within 3–5 weeks
  6. Booking & Payment
    • 30–50% booking deposit to secure the date
    • Remaining balance due before or on the wedding day
